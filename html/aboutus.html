What's Our

Why?

As youth in New Zealand who have dealt with issues regarding contraception and periods, we seek to empower and provide equal access to free contraception and sanitary products. We believe all individuals—regardless of where they come from and how they identify—should have access to reproductive health education.

Accessibility

Although healthcare is already confidential, the act of physically making a consultation is daunting for many young people. Our website allows easy access to confidential phone call consultations for youth when enquiring for both contraception and period products.

Sustainability

Providing immediate access to pre-existing sanitary product systems via Family Planning. Centralising resources on how to create your own products.

Gender Inclusivity

We recognise that people who mensturate and require contraception are of all genders. Therefore, we focus on gender neutral marketing and outreach, such as via social media and communal facilities (e.g. gyms, bathrooms, community centres).
